Lake Ziway, also known as Hora-Dambal, is one of the freshwater Rift Valley lakes in Ethiopia. Let's explore this fascinating lake:
Lake Ziway lies about 100 miles south of Addis Ababa, straddling the border between the Oromia and Southern Nations, Nationalities, and Peoples' Region. The towns of Batu and Hora-Dambal (Lake Zway) are situated along its shoreline. The lake is fed by two rivers: the Meki from the west and the Katar from the east.
